Every clip leaves the app fully finished, not half-done.

These are the same six tools shown on the homepage, applied to one clip in sequence — this is what actually runs between "upload" and "export".

Smart Reframe

Face-tracking with motion smoothing keeps the speaker centered in the 9:16 frame, even across cuts with multiple people talking.

AI Voiceover

Natural Hindi and English neural voices, male and female, several accents — narrates or redubs the clip automatically.

Captions & Text

Auto-generated captions synced to the voiceover, plus logo, text overlays, and shape/blur tools for the frame.

00:19:00 — EXPORT SPECIFICATIONS

What actually comes out the other end.

No vague "high quality" claims — these are the exact format details every export follows.

FORMAT MP4, H.264 video codec — the format YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and TikTok all accept directly, no conversion needed.
ASPECT RATIO 9:16 vertical by default, matched to the source video's own resolution rather than a fixed forced size.
EXPORT SPEED Clips are cut in parallel using your CPU's available threads, and most exports skip re-encoding entirely — a batch from a long video typically finishes in minutes.
WHERE IT RUNS Fully offline for editing and exporting on your own Windows 10/11 PC. Only the AI voiceover step briefly needs an internet connection.
SYSTEM NEEDED 64-bit Windows 10 or 11, 8GB RAM minimum (16GB recommended for longer source videos). No dedicated GPU required — integrated graphics work, a GPU just speeds things up.
YOUR FOOTAGE Never uploaded to a remote server. Every cut, voice pass, and export happens locally, on the machine the app is installed on.
