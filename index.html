00:18:50 — FULL DETAILS

Everything you need to know before buying.

System requirements

Windows 10 or 11, 64-bit. 8GB RAM minimum (16GB recommended for longer source videos). No GPU required — works on integrated graphics, faster with a dedicated GPU. ~2GB free disk space plus space for your source footage and exports.

What's in the box

The full desktop editor: smart reframe, AI voiceover (Hindi + English, multiple voices), auto-captions, speed/zoom/color tools, background music mixing, and parallel export. One installer, no separate add-ons to buy.

Output formats

Exports as MP4 (H.264), vertical 9:16 by default — the format YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and TikTok all accept directly. You upload the finished file yourself.

Internet requirement

Runs fully offline for editing and exporting. Only needs internet briefly for AI voiceover generation (uses a cloud voice engine) — everything else works with no connection.

Support

Email support included with every license. Lifetime plan gets priority response. Reach out any time you're stuck on a specific export or setting.

Who this is for

Creators, podcasters, and educators who already have long-form video (YouTube, podcasts, talks, vlogs) and want to turn it into shorts without hiring an editor or paying per-video clipping fees.

00:26:10 — QUESTIONS

FAQ

Does this download videos from other people's channels? +

No. You upload footage you filmed or already have the rights to. That's the whole workflow.

Does it need a subscription server to run? +

No — it runs locally on your own Windows PC. Your files never leave your machine.

What can I use it for exactly? +

Upload a long video — a podcast episode, vlog, talk, or webinar you own — and the editor finds strong moments, reframes them to vertical, adds captions/voiceover/music, and exports ready-to-post shorts.

Do I need editing experience? +

No. Smart Reframe and auto-captions work automatically on default settings. Manual controls (speed, zoom, color, text) are there if you want more control, but not required to get a usable output.

How long does it take to process a video? +

Depends on your PC and video length, but clips are cut in parallel using all your CPU threads, and most exports don't need re-encoding — so a batch of shorts from a 1-hour video typically finishes in a few minutes, not hours.

What languages does the AI voiceover support? +

Hindi and English, with multiple male/female voices and accent options (India, US, UK) for English.

Can I use one license on two computers? +

Each license is meant for one device. Need it on a second machine? Email support and we'll sort it out.

What if I'm outside India? +

Use the card checkout option — it converts your currency automatically at payment.

What payment methods are accepted? +

UPI, GPay, and PhonePe for India, plus international cards for buyers outside India — all through a secure payment partner. Your card and bank details never pass through us directly.

Is there a refund policy? +

If the app doesn't run on your system after reasonable troubleshooting with support, we'll refund you. Email support with your order details.

Are updates really free? +

Yes, for the life of your license tier — new features roll into the same app with no extra charge.

How do I get the download after paying? +
